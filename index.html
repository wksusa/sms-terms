Nova AI Assistant — WKS Restaurant Group

Program Description

Nova is an AI-powered assistant for WKS Restaurant Group employees. Nova provides support for IT troubleshooting, facilities work order management, and operational inquiries via SMS and voice. Messages may include work order updates, IT ticket confirmations, troubleshooting guidance, and follow-up summaries from voice calls.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your service needs and interactions with Nova. You will only receive messages related to support requests you initiate or ongoing issues you are involved with. No marketing or promotional messages are sent through this program.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your text messaging plan.

Opt-In

By texting Nova's phone number or verbally consenting to receive text messages during a voice call, you agree to receive SMS messages from the Nova AI Assistant program as described in these terms.

Opt-Out

You can opt out at any time by texting STOP to the number from which you received messages. You will receive a one-time confirmation message and will not receive further messages unless you re-subscribe.

Help

For help or more information, text HELP to the number from which you received messages. You will receive a reply with support contact information.
